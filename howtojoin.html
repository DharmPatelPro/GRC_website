How to Join GTU Robotics Club

GTU Robotics Club welcomes passionate students from all branches and years at GTU and its affiliated colleges. If you are curious, committed, and eager to learn robotics hands-on, follow the steps below to become a part of our vibrant community!

Selection Process

  1. Watch for Recruitment Notification: We announce recruitment on our Instagram and college notice boards.
  2. Fill the Application Form: Submit your details and area of interest. (Form link will be shared in the notification.)
  3. Participate in Interview/Task Round: Shortlisted applicants are invited for a basic interview and/or a small task to assess curiosity, commitment, and basic understanding.
