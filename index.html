About Our Club

GRC (Generic Robotics Club) is a community of passionate students dedicated to exploring, learning, and innovating in the field of robotics and technology. We believe in sharing knowledge, building creative solutions, and empowering each other through hands-on projects and collaborative learning. Join us to be a part of an inspiring journey where we learn, create, and teach together!

Achievements

Robocon Finalist 2024

Reached the finals of the national Robocon competition, showcasing our innovative robot design and teamwork.

Best Innovation Award

Awarded for the most creative solution in the TechFest 2023 robotics challenge.

Community Outreach

Organized workshops and seminars to inspire and teach robotics to over 500 students in local schools.
